The system observes customers arriving, checks their tea preferences, monitors milk and sugar levels, controls the cooking process, and prepares tea according to customer requirements.

The AI assistant can decide:

Exam Trick to Remember:

Whenever you see:

"AI system observes, decides, and acts"

Write:

PEAS Part	Ask Yourself
Agent	Who is making decisions?
Environment	Where does it work?
Sensors	What does it observe?
Actuators	What actions can it perform?
Performance	How do we know it is good?
